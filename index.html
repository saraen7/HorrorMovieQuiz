1922 1954 1968 1974

That's wrong!

The horror genre has never gotten much love from the Academy. Though there still seems to be a bias against scary movies during awards season, The Exorcist earned 10 Oscar nominations in 1974, including a Best Supporting Actress nod for Linda Blair, who was just 15 years old at the time.

Which iconic film is the first American film to feature a toilet?

Psycho The Birds Vertigo Dial M for Murder

That's right!

That's wrong!

Psycho is the first American film to show a toilet on screen. It's also the first American film in which we hear a toilet being flushed.

How long into the film does it take for the shark to appear in Jaws?

32 minutes 58 minutes 1 hour and 21 minutes 1 hour and 47 minutes

That's right!

That's wrong!

While the lack of shark appearances until 1 hour and 21 minutes in works to heighten the tension in Jaws, the real reason the shark isn’t shown in full is because the mechanical shark that was built rarely worked during filming. Director Steven Spielberg had to create inventive ways (like Quint’s yellow barrels) to shoot around the non-functional movie shark.
